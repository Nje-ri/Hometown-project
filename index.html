Visit Nairobi

Enjoy the serene environment, culture, and fun in the capital city of Kenya

Top three activities to do in Nairobi

Take a wild Safari

Enjoy watching the wildlife at the Nairobi National Park. Get to see a wide variety of wildlife ranging from lions, elephants, zebras, giraffes, and many more.

Learn about Kenya's Heritage

Explore the archaeological artifacts, ethnographic displays, and various art forms, showcasing the diverse cultural heritage of Kenya.

Play games and have fun

Get to visit various malls in the city where you will get a chance to engage in fun activities and games like bowling, glitch VR, board games, and many more
